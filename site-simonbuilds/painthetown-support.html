Plans & generating

My plans are taking a long time to generate

Real research takes a moment — Paint the Town is checking live venue information for your city. A typical generation takes 2–4 minutes; multi-day trips take longer. You'll get a push notification when plans are ready, so you don't have to watch the screen.

I hit my plan limit — what are the limits?

Free accounts include 3 plan generations per month. Premium includes 15 per month (up to 5 per day) plus vacation planning. Limits reset on the 1st of each month. The counter at the top of the plan screen shows where you stand.

A venue in my plan is closed or wrong

Venue data is researched live, but places change fast. Use the swap feature (tap the stop → swap) to get a replacement that fits the rest of your plan — and always confirm hours with the venue for important nights. If a plan was unusable, email us; we make those right.

How do I stop it suggesting the same places?

Mark plans as scheduled or completed. Venues from those plans go into your taste memory as "been there" and won't be suggested again. Thumbs-down on a stop also teaches it what to avoid.

Why can't I plan a vacation on the free plan?

Multi-day trips take a full research pass for every day of the trip — it's by far the most expensive thing Paint the Town does, so it lives in Premium. Free accounts can still browse destination suggestions.

Account & data

How do I delete my account and data?

Email simonshih@hotmail.com from your account's email address and we'll permanently delete your account, taste profile, and all plans within 30 days. See the privacy policy for details on what we store.

How do I change my taste profile?

Open the Profile tab → Taste Profile. Changes apply to every plan you generate afterward; existing saved plans aren't affected.

Notifications aren't arriving

Check Settings → Notifications → Paint the Town on your iPhone and make sure notifications are allowed. If they're on and still quiet, sign out and back in to refresh your device registration, then generate a plan to test.

Billing

How do subscriptions and refunds work?

Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and managed in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. Canceling keeps Premium active until the end of the paid period.
