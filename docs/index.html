How it works

From "where should we go?" to a full evening, in four steps.

Paint the Town isn't a list of links. Every plan is a schedule — times, durations, addresses, and costs that fit your budget.

✎

Teach it your taste

A short quiz captures what you both love — cuisines, activities, music, drinks — and what to avoid. It keeps learning from every plan you rate.

⌕

AI researches live

Claude searches the real web for currently-operating venues in your city — checking hours for your actual date, prices, and what's happening that night.

☰

Pick from three plans

Three distinct evenings, each fully scheduled and budgeted, with a note on why each stop matches you. No two plans share a venue.

⇄

Remix & make it yours

Mix and match stops across plans, or swap any single stop for something different. Save it, add it to your calendar, share it with your date.

Built for people who hate planning

Every detail handled.

The parts of planning you'd forget, or never think to check — Paint the Town does them on every plan.

Taste memory

It remembers where you've been

Venues from dates you've done never come back. Places you loved shape future picks; places you didn't are avoided.

Weather-aware

Rain plans, automatically

Paint the Town checks the forecast for your date and plans around it — patios when it's beautiful, cozy rooms when it isn't.

Budget-honest

Priced before you go

Every stop carries a realistic cost estimate, and the whole evening is built to fit the budget you set — not blow past it.

Steerable

Vibe & must-haves

Want it romantic? First-date casual? Must include live music and dessert? Set the chips before you generate and the plans obey.

Swap anything

One stop wrong? Fix just that

Swap a single stop and Paint the Town finds a replacement that fits the schedule, the budget, and doesn't clash with the rest of the night.

Ready to go

Calendar & sharing built in

Add the whole evening to your calendar in one tap, or share the itinerary with your date as clean, readable text.

Book it

Reservation links on every stop

Paint the Town captures each venue's real booking page while it researches — OpenTable, Resy, or the restaurant's own site — so reserving is one tap.

Partner mode

Plans that fit you both

Link accounts with your partner and every plan merges both taste profiles — nothing either of you dislikes ever gets planned.

Never forget

Anniversary autopilot & reminders

A weekly date-night nudge on your schedule, and anniversary reminders two weeks out — while the good tables are still open. Surprise mode shares only "be ready at 7."

Questions

FAQ

Are the venues real?

Yes — that's the whole point. Paint the Town uses Claude with live web search to find currently-operating venues and checks them for your actual date. Every stop links to the venue's website so you can verify or book.

How is this different from asking ChatGPT?

Three ways: Paint the Town plans against your saved taste profile and everywhere you've already been; it produces a scheduled, budgeted itinerary rather than a list of suggestions; and it's built for the follow-through — remixing, swapping stops, calendar, and sharing.

Does it work in my city?

Paint the Town works anywhere web search does — big cities shine, but it does honest work in smaller towns too. Type any city; there's no fixed coverage list.

What about my data?

Your taste profile and plans live in your account and are used only to plan for you. No ads, no selling data, no third-party tracking. Details in the privacy policy.

When can I get it?

Paint the Town is in private beta on iOS now, with an App Store launch to follow. Email us to join the beta.
